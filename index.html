The Museum of Music of Azerbaijan is a modern, interactive and inviting cultural center where you can see national instruments and learn their history. Read about and listen to great composers.

The official logo of the museum.

Only in our museum national ensembles perform every weekend. Here you can listen live to wonderful music on the tar, hear how Ashugs perform mugham. We also often invite young and talented people and give them the opportunity to perform their music at a concert in our museum!
